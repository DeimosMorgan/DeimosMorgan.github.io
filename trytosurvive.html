Try to survive

In this game players must try to reach to the last arena. With every new arena it becomes more difficult. But for facilitation of task a player needs to win an opponent and collect coins. As soon as a player will have enough coins, he will be able to buy to itself a new weapon that more powerful than previous. This game was made for android. This game was made on the engine of Unity
